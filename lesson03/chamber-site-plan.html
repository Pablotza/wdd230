Buenos Aires Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Buenos Aires Chamber of Commerce serves as a hub for promoting economic development, fostering business relationships, and providing valuable resources to the local community.

Scenarios

1. How can local restaurants and food businesses collaborate with the chamber to promote their offerings?

2. Is there a calendar of events supported by the chamber, especially those related to cultural celebrations or festivals?

3. Where can I find information about historical districts and landmarks in Buenos Aires?

Color Schema

Main Color: #75AADB (Sky Blue)

Accent Color: #FFFFFF (White)

Hover Color: #FFD100 (Yellow)

Typography

Main Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif (used for headings and body text)
